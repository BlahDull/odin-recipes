Stew

Description

A classical dish that has been prepared by peasants for generations An easy way to get rid of spare ingredients and make something that tastes good. It's... full of vegetables so kinda healthy? I guess?

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Stew meat
  • Carrots
  • Tomatoes
  • Seasonings
  • Any other stuff you have... I guess

Cook Steps

  1. Chop potatoes
  2. Prepare stew meat if necessary (defrost)
  3. Dump everything into pot
  4. Add water to pot and season it up
  5. Boil and cook it for like... an hour and a half?
  6. Eat it up
