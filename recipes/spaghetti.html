Spaghetti

Description

A classic yet delicious dish that has been prepared by italians for generations. Tastes so good yet is so easy to prepare. Like so easy. It's also super healthy! Full of tomato! Yum!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Seasonings

Cook Steps

  1. Put a lot of those noodles into boiling water! The water has to already be boiling! Boil 'em for around 8 mins!
  2. Once they are done... slather them in that tomato sauce!
  3. Cook that ground beef as well
  4. Season the stuff and add it to the spaghetti
  5. Eat it up.
