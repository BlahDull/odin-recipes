Chicken Wrap

Description

A tasty and healthy dish that not only looks good, but is packed full of nutrients and vitamins! This is due to the yummy avocado... the tasty tomato and the yummy chicken.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Avocado
  • Tortilla
  • Skinless boneless chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Other stuff you want to add

Cooking Steps

  1. Begin by boiling the chicken
  2. Then prepare the guacamole with the avocado and tomato
  3. Salt the chicken water? Idk what to call it
  4. Salt the guacamole
  5. Once the chicken is done, shred it up!
  6. Put some guacamole and chicken in a tortilla and wrap it up!
  7. Eat it up!
